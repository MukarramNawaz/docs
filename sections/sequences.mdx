In the “Sequences” section, you can create and manage the process that will enable you to send, delay, and message, as well as integrate the AI agent.

1. Go to your organization in the top left-hand corner.
2. Click on **“Sequences”**.
3. Then click on **“Publish Sequence”**.

### Adding Action Steps

- Click on the **“+”** and select **“add action step”**.
- On the right, select the source **“WhatsApp”** and click on **“continue”**.
- Select **“WhatsApp account #1”** and click on **“continue”**.

### Template Creation

In the **template** section:
- Select the language for the message.
- Under **“message”**, write the template for the first message the person will receive (you can use emojis).
- Click on **“continue”**.

### Adding Conversation Conditions

- Click on the **“+”** below **“action”** and select **“Add condition”**.
- Click on **“filter (condition)”** to define your conversation conditions.
- Click on **“confirm”**.

### Creating Sending Deadlines

- Click on **“+”**.
- Select **“Add delay”**—a tab will appear on the right.
- Fill in the time you want to send a message or reply.
- Click on **“confirm”**.

### Publishing Your Sequence

- In the top right-hand corner, click on the small circle to **ACTIVATE** the sequence—this is very important for usability.
- Click on **“publish sequence”**.
- Then validate by clicking on **“continue”**.
