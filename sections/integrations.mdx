The **Integrations** section lets you link CSVs, networks, Zapier, and more to your application for enhanced functionality.

## Navigation

To access the **Integrations** menu:

- Click on **“Integrations”** in the bottom-left corner.
- Then, select **“Browse Integrations.”**

## Connecting WhatsApp

To connect **WhatsApp**:

1. Click on **“WhatsApp”** and then **“Connect WhatsApp.”**
2. Click on **“Create WhatsApp Profile.”**
3. Fill in your details and click **“Confirm and Continue.”**
4. Click on **“Connect WhatsApp Number.”**
5. Once back in [app.orsay.ai](http://app.orsay.ai/), click **“Create Inbound Message.”**
6. Enter the message you want and click **“Finish and Create Link.”**

Your WhatsApp profile is successfully integrated.

## Modifying Integrations

To modify an integration:

1. Click on the **“...”** next to the organization in question.
2. Select **“Edit.”**
3. Make the necessary changes.
4. Click **“Save.”**

<img src="/images/integrations/Capture_decran_2025-01-08_a_11.54.36.png" alt="Modify Integrations" />

## Deleting Integrations

To delete an integration:

1. Click on the **“...”** next to the organization in question.
2. Select **“Delete.”**
3. Confirm by clicking **“Remove Agent.”**

### Deleting a Phone Number

1. Go to the **Organization** section.
2. Navigate to **“Link Phone Number.”**
3. Click the red trash icon.
4. Confirm by clicking **“Remove Number.”**

## Uploading Files

To upload a file to the dashboard:

1. Click on **“Bulk Leads”** and then **“Upload Files.”**
2. Import the CSV file.
3. Fill in the country (e.g., "France" for French entries).
4. Match column titles for **First Name** and **Phone Number.**
5. Select the appropriate sequence.
6. Click on **“Upload File.”**

> Note: To upload a CSV, ensure the sequence uses the trigger type `CONTACT_SUBSCRIBED_TO_SEQUENCE.`

## Updating Auto-Run Schedule

To update the auto-run schedule:

1. Select the **Upload Sequence.**
2. Enter the **number of leads** to add to the sequence.
3. Click on **“Update Schedule.”**
